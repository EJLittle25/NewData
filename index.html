    Where does this happiness originate though?

    The following graph intends to show this

    With the strength of family on the x-axis, the country's "happiness rank" on the y-axis, and the size of the circles corresponding the economic GDP per capita, a trend can be seen.

    As the family strength and economic GDP per capita increases within a country, the country's "happiness rank" increases. This means that maybe money does buy happiness and home and happiness is where the heart is.
